Company Profile

ཚོང་ཁང་གི་ངོ་སྤྲོད།

西藏炫音音响销售有限公司成立于2015年05月15日，注册地位于西藏拉萨市，法定代表人为任勇。公司自成立以来，始终致力于提供高品质的音视频系统解决方案，涵盖舞台灯光、酒吧KTV、朗玛厅、慢摇吧、演艺中心等多种场景。

Tibet Xuanyin Audio Sales Co., Ltd. was established on May 15, 2015, with its registered address in Lhasa, Tibet. The legal representative is Ren Yong. Since its establishment, the company has been committed to providing high-quality audio-visual system solutions for various scenarios including stage lighting, bar KTV, Langma Hall, slow shake bars, and performance centers.
